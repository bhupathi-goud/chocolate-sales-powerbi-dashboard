#  Chocolate Sales Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing chocolate product sales across 
multiple geographies, sales persons, and product categories.

## Key Metrics
-  Total Revenue: $136.08M
-  Total Boxes Shipped: 8M
-  Total Profit: $78M
-  Profit %: 57.34%
-  Total Shipments: 24K

## 🔍 Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards** — Revenue, Boxes, Profit, Profit %, Shipments
- **Amount CY vs PY** — Current vs previous year revenue trend
- **Boxes CY vs PY** — Current vs previous year volume trend
- **Shipment Distribution** — Histogram of shipment sizes
- **Amount by Geo** — Revenue breakdown by country (donut chart)
- **Top 5 Sales Persons** — Ranked by revenue with profit %
- **Top 6 Products** — Best performing products by revenue
- **Products Table** — Full product list with profit % indicators
